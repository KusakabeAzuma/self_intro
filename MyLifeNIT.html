Here I would like to look back on the five years I spent at NIT Tsuyama.
My memories of my freshman year are about dormitory life. It was difficult to do many things for the first time.
However, my friends who overcame the same difficulties are still my friends today.
We were scared the guidance dorm students.
My memory from my second year is learning about programming in the information systems department.
The third year laboratory training was very difficult. Especially the handwritten reports were very time consuming.
Therefore, I stayed up all night every Sunday.

The photo above shows the award I received when I was in my third year.

Now that I am a fifth grade, I have begun my job search and am spending every day in a fulfilling way.
However, I am tired of all the things that have been happening lately.
I will do my best every day without overexerting myself so as not to damage my body.
